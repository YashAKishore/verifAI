Classic Shepherd’s Pie

A warm, comforting dish made with seasoned lamb, vegetables, and creamy mashed potato topping.

Ingredients

500g minced lamb

1 onion, diced

2 carrots

1 cup peas

Tomato paste

Potatoes + butter

Cooking Steps

1. Brown lamb in pan

2. Add vegetables

3. Simmer with sauce

4. Mash potatoes

5. Bake until golden

Food Safety Tips

Always cook meat thoroughly and avoid cross-contamination in kitchens.
